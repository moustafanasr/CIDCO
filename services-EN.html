Frequently Asked Questions

What is the project implementation timeframe?

The implementation timeframe varies depending on the nature and complexity of the project. Generally, it ranges from 4 to 12 weeks for medium projects. We provide a detailed timeline after studying the project requirements.

Do you provide services for individuals or companies only?

We provide services to both individuals and companies. Whether you're an independent artisan, own a small or medium-sized project, or even a large company, we have solutions specifically designed for your needs.

What is the cost of services?

The cost varies depending on the type of service and its complexity. We provide transparent quotes after assessing your needs. We also offer multiple packages to suit different budgets.

Do you provide support after project completion?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive support package after project completion that includes performance monitoring, periodic consultations, and technical support as needed. The support duration varies depending on the chosen package.
