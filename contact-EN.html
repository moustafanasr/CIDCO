Frequently Asked Questions

What are the available contact methods?

You can contact us by phone (+20 109 808 0087), email (info@cidco.tech), or by filling out the contact form on this page. You can also visit our main office in Nasr City during working hours.

What are your working hours?

Official working hours are from Sunday to Thursday from 8 AM to 5 PM. We receive phone calls during this period, and you can send emails at any time and we will respond within 24 hours.

How long does it take to respond to inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible. Phone calls are usually answered immediately during working hours, emails are responded to within 24 hours, and contact forms are responded to within 48 hours.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we provide an initial free consultation for all customers interested in our services. You can schedule a consultation by phone or by filling out the contact form and selecting "Service Inquiry".
