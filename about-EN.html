Who We Are

Creative Industries Development Company (CIDCO) is a leading company in supporting and developing creative industries in Saudi Arabia and the region. The company was established with an ambitious vision to enhance the position of creative industries locally and internationally.

We believe that creativity is the main driver of the modern economy, and we strive to create a supportive environment for creators in various fields by providing comprehensive and integrated solutions that meet their needs and aspirations.

Thanks to our team of experts and specialists, we provide consulting and development services that help artisans and small and medium enterprises grow and compete in local and international markets.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading company in developing creative industries locally and regionally, and to contribute to transforming Saudi Arabia into a regional center for distinguished creative industries.

Our Mission

Empowering creators and small and medium enterprises in the field of creative industries by providing integrated solutions that include development, quality, marketing, and access to global markets, while preserving cultural identity and authenticity.
